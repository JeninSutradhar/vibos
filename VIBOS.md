### **Services Provided**

Our services include prospect entry and AMS updates, quoting for new business and renewals, policy servicing support, binding assistance, payment processing support, documentation management, and maintaining accurate client and policy records within the agency management system (AMS).
* **Who is this for:** This service is designed for insurance agencies, insurance brokers, MGAs (Managing General Agents), and insurance service providers who need reliable back-office support to handle operational tasks efficiently.
* **Benefits:** Clients benefit from reduced operational costs, faster turnaround time, improved data accuracy, and the ability to focus on sales and client relationships instead of administrative tasks. Our support helps agencies increase productivity, manage higher workloads, and scale their operations without hiring additional in-house staff.
* **Tools/Technology:** Yes, we work with commonly used Agency Management Systems (AMS) and other insurance tools such as Applied Epic, AMS360, HawkSoft, EZLynx, Vertafore, and carrier portals. We also use collaboration and productivity tools like CRM systems, cloud-based document management platforms, and secure communication tools to ensure efficient and secure workflow management.

### **Outsourcing Model**

* **How Clients Work With You:** Clients can work with us through flexible engagement models based on their business needs: Dedicated Staff Model: Clients can hire a dedicated virtual assistant or back-office specialist who works exclusively with their team and handles daily operational tasks. Monthly Outsourcing Model: Businesses can outsource specific functions such as insurance back-office support, bookkeeping, digital marketing, or recruitment on a fixed monthly plan. Per-Task / Project-Based Pricing: Clients can assign specific tasks or short-term projects like quoting, data entry, AMS updates, or administrative work and pay per task or project. Virtual Assistant Model: Clients can hire a remote virtual assistant as a personal administrative support professional to manage emails, scheduling, documentation, research, and other daily business tasks.
* **Dedicated vs. Shared Team:** Clients are provided with a dedicated employee who works exclusively for their business, similar to an in-house team member but operating remotely. The dedicated staff member integrates with the client’s existing team, follows their processes and workflows, and provides consistent support for daily tasks.

* **Typical Onboarding Process:**
  * **Initial Consultation:** We schedule a call with the client to understand their business, services, workload, and the type of support they require.
  * **Process Understanding & Requirement Analysis:** We review the client’s workflows, tools (such as AMS or CRM), and specific tasks they want to outsource. This helps us clearly define the scope of work.
  * **Team Assignment & Setup:** A dedicated virtual assistant or specialist is assigned to the client. Necessary system access, communication channels, and security protocols are set up.
  * **Training & Workflow Alignment:** The assigned team member learns the client’s processes, guidelines, and expectations to ensure smooth integration with the client’s existing team.
  * **Work Begins & Ongoing Support:** Once everything is set up, work officially begins. We maintain regular communication, progress tracking, and performance monitoring to ensure high-quality service and continuous improvement.
